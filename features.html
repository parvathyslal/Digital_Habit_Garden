01

Interactive Habit Tracking

The application includes a dynamic habit tracking system where users can create, manage, and monitor their daily habits through an intuitive interface. Each completed habit contributes experience points (XP) and progression within the garden environment. The system is designed to encourage consistency by making productivity visually meaningful rather than repetitive or mechanical.

02

Dynamic Garden Growth

One of the core features of the project is the animated growth mechanism of the virtual garden.At the initial stage,the garden may contain only a seed or small sprout. As users continue completing habits regularly, the plant gradually develops into: a young sapling, a flowering plant, and eventually a fully grown tree.These animations make the progression feel alive and emotionally rewarding, reinforcing the user's motivation to maintain habits consistently.

03

Live Theme & UI Changes

To increase personalization and user engagement, the application includes a theme selection system during login. Users can choose one among four unique environmental themes based on their mood or personal preference. Each theme introduces a completely different atmosphere through customized: color palettes, background designs, environmental effects, animations, and visual assets.This feature ensures that every user experiences a unique and immersive environment while interacting with the application.

04

Streaks & Rituals

A gentle counter never a punishment. Days stack into weeks, weeks into quiet rituals, and the small flame of consistency grows on its own.To make habit completion more satisfying, the application incorporates visual reward mechanisms and celebratory animations whenever users achieve progress milestones. Another important feature is personalized progression based on the selected theme. Although users may reach the same level numerically, the visual rewards and environmental changes differ depending on the chosen theme.
